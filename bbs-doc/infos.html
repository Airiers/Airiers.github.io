What is

BBS MOD (Blockbuster Studio Mod) is a machinima-focused and animation Minecraft mod created by McHorse.

It was made for Fabric 1.20.4 and 1.20.1, but can be used on Forge 1.20.1 with Sinytra Connector mod (with some bugs). Some peoples have successfully installed it on phones, but it did not designed for that, and there are many bugs.

It's the successor of Blockbuster Mod, also made by McHorse for Forge 1.10.2, 1.11.2 and 1.12.2.
